BUSINESS SCOPE

As EFR Group expertise is for logistics & supply chain services, EFR Trading primarily focused to offer Trade House services to customers as value added service to our Logistics services. We have started to provide end to end logistics solutions including the trade formality transactions such as T/T and L/C arrangements. It covers stock handling, reporting, pick up & delivery as well as cash collection & remittance matters.

Later on, as demand from the market and new opportunities emerged, we started our own trading activities relating to the agriculture sector in Myanmar.

EFR Trading is offering services to the renowned customers in Myanmar. The company ensures to sustain and develop more business based on our strong cooperation with local customers and oversea partner companies.

EFR Trading is setting up the regional branch offices in Myanmar and focus on national collection/distribution and e-commerce activities to fully serve a the 5th Party Logistics Services in Myanmar.
